Formats

We have two formats namely "Connect with the Mentor" and "Shadow the Mentor", to connect the Rotarators with the Mentors(Rotarians) from various classifications of your Choice and Passion.

Format 1

Connect with the Mentor

Great to interact with the Mentor from the Classification you are passionate, to advance your thoughts towards success.

  • Apply, Review, Matching
    • Applying: Select your Classification.
    • Review: Our team will Verify the Rotaractor's identity.
    • Review: Our team will review the application.
    • Mathcing: Our team will coordinate and finalise the Mentorship Session
  • / Connect: Gather Knowledge and insights in a 1:1 session (40 mins - 60 mins).
  • Feedback: Share your feedbak to help us improve RotaSambandh.

Format 2

Shadow the Mentor

Spend a day in the life of the Mentor in the Classification of you choice. Gain insights on the realworld industry experience.

  • Apply, Review, Matching
    • Applying: Select your Classification.
    • Review: Our team will Verify the Rotaractor's identity.
    • Review: Our team will review the application.
    • Mathcing: Our team will coordinate and finalise the Mentorship Session
  • Shadow: Travel a day in the life of the Mentor to gather realworld industry experience.
  • Feedback: Share your feedbak to help us improve RotaSambandh.
